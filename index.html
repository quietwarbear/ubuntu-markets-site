Our Foundation

Ubuntu"I am because we are."

Ubuntu is not a word — it is a way of being. It is the belief that our humanity is bound together. That no one rises alone. That a community's strength is the measure of its care for one another.

Everything we build begins here. Every product, every feature, every decision asks the same question: does this serve the village?

About Us

Built from the
inside out.

Ubuntu Markets (LLC) and Ubuntu Villages (501c3) are Black-owned organizations co-founded by Hodari and Shylah Touré in Oakland, CA. Together they form a single ecosystem — commercial innovation and community purpose operating in alignment.

We don't build apps for demographics. We build tools for families, for circles, for the village — technology that carries the weight and warmth of lived experience.
